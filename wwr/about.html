History

Our company was founded in 2024 with a passion for exploring the thrill of whitewater rafting. We began with a small fleet of 21 rafts and a team of experienced guides. Over the years, we have grown to become a leading provider of whitewater rafting adventures in Kenya. Our commitment to safety, environmental responsibility, and unforgettable experiences has earned us a reputation as a trusted name in the industry. We are proud to have introduced countless people to the excitement of navigating the rapids and the beauty of the surrounding wilderness.
